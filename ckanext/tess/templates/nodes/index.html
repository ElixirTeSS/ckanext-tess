    ELIXIR Hub

    The Hub accommodates the ELIXIR executive management and secretariat and coordinates integration of services run from the ELIXIR nodes. It has overall responsibility for developing and delivering the ELIXIR programme, coordinates ELIXIR's governance bodies and technical committees, and provides oversight of biological data throughout Europe by creating data registries and coordinating development of data standards.
