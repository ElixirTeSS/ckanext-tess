ELIXIR is a pan-European research infrastructure for biological information. It consists of the Hub and a number of ELIXIR nodes. Each participating country have their own node. The only exception is the UK, which has two - with EMBL-EBI being a node on its own. You can add a new node here if your country has signed the declaration to join ELIXIR. Please be very thorough with the information you provide.
