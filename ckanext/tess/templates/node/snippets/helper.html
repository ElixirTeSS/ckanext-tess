ELIXIR is a pan-European research infrastructure. It consists of the Hub and a number of ELIXIR nodes. Each participating node has a head of node, a training co-ordinator, and a technical co-ordinator. You can add a new node here if your country has signed the declaration to join ELIXIR. Please be very thorough with the information you provide.
