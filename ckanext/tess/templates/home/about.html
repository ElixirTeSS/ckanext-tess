    TeSS (ELIXIR Training eSupport System) is ELIXIR's training portal, developed by ELIXIR-UK, for discovering, packaging and linking training resources aggregated from the plethora of fragmented training resources in life sciences. The training resources are provided by various ELIXIR nodes across Europe and other training providers world-wide.

    New technologies are generating huge amounts of life sciences data that scientists now need to transform into new discoveries. The problem is that very few researchers have all the right skills to take advantage of this sudden data bonanza.

    As part of the pan-European ELIXIR project, ELIXIR-UK is responding to this challenge by taking a UK and European lead in training research scientists in computing, statistics and bioinformatics. By plugging the skills gap we aim to strengthen science across Europe, and will set the agenda for training needs in the future.

    FAQ

    • Is TeSS a repository of materials?

      No. The TeSS is an aggregator of ELIXIR-relevant training information, harvested from individual ELIXIR Nodes and from third-party training content providers.

      TeSS does not store training materials itself, rather it stores links to original training materials and also enables users to create their own packages and workflows of (linked) materials to suit their needs.

    • What is the main difference between the TeSS and GOBLET’s Training Portal?

      TeSS harvests training information from ELIXIR Nodes and from 3-rd providers, and aggregates that information according to different ‘views’ (e.g., ELIXIR overview versus Node-specific) - it does not store training materials locally. The GOBLET Training Portal acts a repository of materials and courses, and provides a catalogue of and trainers and course organisers. The primary focus of the TeSS is on ELIXIR resources and Nodes; the principal focus of GOBLET is on its subscribed members and their materials/courses.

    • Do I have to upload training materials to TeSS manually?

      Training information is brought into TeSS from various content providers automatically using their APIs (or, where these do not exist, can be scraped automatically from providers’ websites).

      If you would like your content provider to be added to TeSS, please contact the TeSS team so we can arrange a scraper for your site. Alternatively, all of these can be manually added and curated by registered users.

    • How can I add a content provider?

      Registered users can add a content provider manually from content providers page, by clicking the 'Add content provider' button. They will be presented with a form to write a short description, upload the provider's logo and provide a link to provider's website.

      Alternatively, content provider can be added automatically from a script at the time of ingesting training material content from that provider.

    • How can I add a training material?

      Registered users can add training material manually from training materials page, by clicking the 'Add training material' button. They will be presented with a multi-step form to fill in various fields and link to resources. They can also attribute this training material to a particular content provider.

      Alternatively, training material can be added automatically from a script ingesting training material from a particular content provider. Typically, such a script would use the content provider's API, if such exists, or TeSS development team writes a script that scrapes the training materials off provider's website.

    • How do I create a package (or a workflow) and add training material it?

      This is a two step process. Firstly, you have to create, say, a package from the packages pages. Secondly, you have to add an existing training material to that package (note that one training material can belong to several packages and/or workflows). This is done by navigating to the page of that particular training material, then selecting the 'Packages' tab, and selecting the appropriate package from a drop-down list.

    Software

    TeSS is implemented by extending the CKAN platform, an Open Source Data Portal software.

    TeSS is populated automatically by scraping content providers for their training materials. Both the TeSS Portal and all the scraping/aggregation scripts are open source and are released under the GPL license. The source code be found in GitHub.

    Please note that the TeSS at present is in pre-alpha state (as of 21th January 2015). Our first official alpha release is scheduled for the 30th March 2015.
