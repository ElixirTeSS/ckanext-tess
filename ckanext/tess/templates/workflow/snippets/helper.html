{{ _('What are workflows?') }}

Training workflow is a visual representation of a collection of training materials that can help navigating through the available resources specific to your research and training needs.

For example, you can use workflows to represent a step-by-step breakdown of typical stages of some kind of data analysis (e.g., a protein sequence analysis pipeline, structure analysis protocol, etc.), where each node in the workflow represents an analysis step and links to the relevant training resources.
