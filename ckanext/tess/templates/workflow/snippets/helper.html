{{ _('What are workflows?') }}

{% trans %} Training workflows are collections of training materials that people need to familiarise themselves with in order to learn about a certain analysis or process, e.g. structure prediction, homology modelling, protein ligand docking, MS-based metabolomic analysis, etc.

Similar to steps in the actual analysis, these collections of materials are organized in a particular order, so people can follow this predefined order or can skip steps they are already familiar with and dive into steps they want to learn more about. {% endtrans %}
